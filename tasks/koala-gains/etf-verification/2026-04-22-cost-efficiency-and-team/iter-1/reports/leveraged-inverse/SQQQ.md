Strengths of this ETF include its high daily dollar volume of `2463772602` and tightly managed `0.02%` bid-ask spread, which enable optimal entry and exit pricing. The primary risk is the structural financing and internal friction cost inherent to its -3x daily mandate, which easily exceeds the `0.95%` sticker fee. Additional risk arises from attempting to hold the position for extended periods, as daily rebalancing structurally erodes capital. Overall, this ETF's cost profile looks mixed because pristine trading liquidity is heavily counterbalanced by the severe internal costs required to maintain daily leveraged exposure.
