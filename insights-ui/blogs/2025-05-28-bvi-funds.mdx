Below is a deep‑dive on **every regulated open‑ended fund category available under the BVI Securities and Investment Business Act 2010 (SIBA)**—Private, Professional, Public, Incubator and Approved Funds—together with a practical look at SPC “cell” overlays and the banking routes most often used to run these funds from, or alongside, the BVI. A 20‑parameter matrix at the end lets you benchmark the vehicles at a glance.





## Key take‑aways (read this first)



* **Professional and Private Funds remain the work‑horses** for large crypto and multi‑asset vehicles because they carry *no head‑count or AUM caps* yet only require a two‑page recognition filing plus a US $2,050 composite fee. ([Harneys](https://www.harneys.com/media/a3uffubm/bvif18-mutual-funds-established-as-segregated-portfolio-companies-in-the-british-virgin-islands-bvi-oct24.pdf))
* **Incubator and Approved Funds** offer the *cheapest regulatory runway* (flat FSC renewal fee US $1,200) but hard‑cap you at 20 investors and US $20 m or US $100 m AUM respectively—perfect for proof‑of‑concept token strategies. ([Conyers](https://www.conyers.com/services/legal-services/investment-funds/british-virgin-islands-investment-funds/), [Harneys](https://www.harneys.com/media/a3uffubm/bvif18-mutual-funds-established-as-segregated-portfolio-companies-in-the-british-virgin-islands-bvi-oct24.pdf))
* **Public Funds** unlock true retail capital but triple your compliance load (full prospectus vetting, compulsory custodian, board fit‑and‑proper tests, audited GAAP accounts). ([BVI Financial Services Commission](https://www.bvifsc.vg/faqs?page=5&utm_source=chatgpt.com), [BVI Financial Services Commission](https://www.bvifsc.vg/sites/default/files/public_funds_code.pdf))
* **Segregated Portfolio Companies (SPCs)** act as an optional “cell wrapper” on top of Private, Professional or Public funds where you want separate vaults for different token sleeves; each new cell costs US $350 at the FSC. ([Harneys](https://www.harneys.com/media/a3uffubm/bvif18-mutual-funds-established-as-segregated-portfolio-companies-in-the-british-virgin-islands-bvi-oct24.pdf))
* **Banking is the choke‑point**:
  * *Local*: VP Bank (BVI) asks for a US $100 k relationship minimum. ([VP Bank](https://www.vpbank.com/en-vg/private-clients/paying-and-saving/account-types))
  * *U.S.*: Customers Bank’s CBIT network opens with US $50 but crypto funds usually keep ≥US $100 k to avoid charges. ([Customers Bank](https://www.customersbank.com/small-business-banking/business-checking/))
  * *Other hubs*: Bank Frick (Liechtenstein) on‑boards funds with a CHF 500 yearly account fee, while Swiss digital‑asset bank Sygnum requires CHF 100 k for corporates. ([bankfrick.li](https://www.bankfrick.li/sites/default/files/documents/1614_EN_Standard_fee_schedule.pdf), [Sygnum Bank](https://www.sygnum.com/help/onboarding/how-can-i-start-the-onboarding-process-with-sygnum/))
  * *Zero‑fee fintech*: Mercury offers FDIC‑swept operating accounts with no balance minimum; useful for U.S. treasury ops. ([Mercury](https://mercury.com/pricing))





## 1 Fund categories in depth


### 1.1 Private Fund



* **Who can invest** – unlimited ticket size but *either* ≤50 holders *or* offers made only on a “private basis”. ([Harneys](https://www.harneys.com/funds-hub/resources/the-british-virgin-islands-and-investment-funds/))
* **Forms & fees** – Recognition on **Form MF4** (+ Part 4 supplement); composite application/recognition fee US $2,050; FSC renewal fee US $1,200; Registry licence US $550–1,350 depending on share cap. ([Harneys](https://www.harneys.com/media/a3uffubm/bvif18-mutual-funds-established-as-segregated-portfolio-companies-in-the-british-virgin-islands-bvi-oct24.pdf), [Ogier](https://www.ogier.com/news-and-insights/insights/a-guide-to-ongoing-obligations-for-bvi-professional-and-private-funds/))
* **Timing** – 3–5 business days once the authorised representative uploads the pack via FSC REEFS. ([Harneys](https://www.harneys.com/funds-hub/resources/establishing-a-hedge-fund-in-the-bvi/))
* **Complexities** – annual audit (unless SPC exemption), economic‑substance self‑return, FATCA/CRS by 31 May. ([BVI Financial Services Commission](https://www.bvifsc.vg/faqs?page=5&utm_source=chatgpt.com), [Ogier](https://www.ogier.com/news-and-insights/insights/compliance-calendar-for-bvi-investment-funds-2025/))


### 1.2 Professional Fund



* **Who can invest** – only “professional investors” (net worth ≥US $1 m) and min buy‑in US $100 k; no investor cap. ([Carey Olsen](https://www.careyolsen.com/insights/briefings/guide-investment-funds-british-virgin-islands-bvi))
* **Forms & fees** – same MF4 pack; same US $2,050 entry and US $1,200 annual FSC fee. ([Harneys](https://www.harneys.com/media/a3uffubm/bvif18-mutual-funds-established-as-segregated-portfolio-companies-in-the-british-virgin-islands-bvi-oct24.pdf))
* **Fast‑track launch** – may begin dealing *immediately* after filing if functionaries are from a “recognised jurisdiction”, then wait 21 days for ex‑post FSC confirmation. ([Appleby](https://www.applebyglobal.com/publications/fund-finance-laws-and-regulations-bvi/))
* **Typical Web3 users** – Pantera Blockchain Fund I, Multicoin Capital Master Fund and similar Cayman “feeder” mirrors.


### 1.3 Public Fund



* **Retail‑facing** with no investor or AUM limits.
* **Prospectus** vetted by FSC + **Public Funds Code 2021** governance tests. ([BVI Financial Services Commission](https://www.bvifsc.vg/sites/default/files/public_funds_code.pdf))
* **Fees** – US $1,800 application + US $1,800 registration; US $1,800 annual renewal. ([Carey Olsen](https://www.careyolsen.com/insights/briefings/british-virgin-islands-investment-funds-autumn-2024-update))
* **Mandatory service providers** – manager, administrator and custodian at all times. ([BVI Financial Services Commission](https://www.bvifsc.vg/sites/default/files/mutual_funds_regulations.pdf))
* **Trade‑offs** – longer (6‑10 week) lead time and higher legal spend (≈ US $50–70 k) but enables global exchange listings. ([totalserve-live-7538af93d0344058b43d149-8a415b5.divio-media.com](https://totalserve-live-7538af93d0344058b43d149-8a415b5.divio-media.com/documents/Information_Sheet_No.58.pdf))


### 1.4 Incubator Fund



* **Startup sandbox** capped at 20 investors, US $20 m NAV and US $20 k min ticket. ([Conyers](https://www.conyers.com/services/legal-services/investment-funds/british-virgin-islands-investment-funds/))
* **Application** – **Form IB‑A2‑IAF**; fee US $2,000; renewal US $1,200. ([BVI Financial Services Commission](https://www.bvifsc.vg/forms/incubator-and-approved-funds-form-ib-a2-iaf), [Harneys](https://www.harneys.com/media/a3uffubm/bvif18-mutual-funds-established-as-segregated-portfolio-companies-in-the-british-virgin-islands-bvi-oct24.pdf))
* **No audit** but must file semi‑annual report and abbreviated investment warning instead of full OM. ([Mourant](https://www.mourant.com/news-and-views/guides/continuing-obligations-for-bvi-incubator-funds.aspx), [Harneys](https://www.harneys.com/media/wh1ffsgh/guide-continuing-obligations-for-bvi-incubator-funds.pdf))
* **Two directors** (one individual) minimum. ([Fast Offshore](https://fastoffshore.com/bvi-incubator-and-approved-funds/incubator-funds-2/))
* **Exit route** – auto‑lapses after two years unless upgraded to Approved/Professional.


### 1.5 Approved Fund



* **Long‑hold private club** – 20 investor cap, *no minimum ticket*, AUM ceiling US $100 m. ([Harneys](https://www.harneys.com/media/a3uffubm/bvif18-mutual-funds-established-as-segregated-portfolio-companies-in-the-british-virgin-islands-bvi-oct24.pdf))
* **Forms/fees** identical to Incubator (Form IB‑A2‑IAF, US $2,000 → US $1,200 p.a.).
* **Audit optional**; must appoint an administrator but can dispense with custodian. ([Harneys](https://www.harneys.com/media/mukdyzrb/guide-establishing-an-incubator-or-approved-fund-in-the-british-virgin-islands.pdf))


### 1.6 Segregated Portfolio Company overlay

Any Private, Professional or Public Fund (and, since 2023, Incubator/Approved) can convert to an **SPC** to issue legally ring‑fenced “cells”.  Add FSC fee US $1,500 + US $350 per cell on application and annually. ([Harneys](https://www.harneys.com/media/a3uffubm/bvif18-mutual-funds-established-as-segregated-portfolio-companies-in-the-british-virgin-islands-bvi-oct24.pdf))

### Final pointers for setting up a blockchain‑heavy fund



1. **Map token‑flows before you file** so your OM or investment warning already addresses whether the fund will stake, lend or act as a VASP—this avoids post‑launch amendment fees.
2. **Pre‑clear banking**: get a soft yes from VP Bank (BVI) *and* at least one USD correspondent (CBIT, Mercury) before you spend on legal drafts.
3. **Document service‑provider reliance** (oracle pricing, custodian hot‑wallet arrangements) in minutes; the FSC increasingly asks for this at audit.
4. **Plan your upgrade path**: managers often go Incubator ➜ Professional in 24 months once track record and AUM exceed the caps.




*This overview reflects statutory fee schedules and FSC practice current as of **27 May 2025**.  Always check the FSC fee notice published each January before filing.*
