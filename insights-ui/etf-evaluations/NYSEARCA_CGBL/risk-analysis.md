The main tradeoff is performance: the conservative posture directly results in weaker absolute returns during pure bull markets. From a retail perspective, an ETF like this serves best as a defensive anchor rather than a primary growth engine. Overall, this ETF's risk profile looks strong because it effectively limits downside exposure while cleanly delivering on the structural expectations of a balanced mandate.
