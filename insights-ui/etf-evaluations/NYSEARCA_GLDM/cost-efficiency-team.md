## Report

The fund's holding fee places it favorably against older physical gold competitors that typically range between `0.15%` and `0.25%`. Liquidity is immense; supported by the previously noted asset base and robust daily trading activity hitting `$274M` in dollar volume, retail investors face nominal execution friction perfectly aligned with the tightest executing commodity trusts. As a physical bullion grantor trust, the portfolio delivers pure, unhedged exposure with a `100%` weight in actual metal bars.

Because it simply vaults metal, portfolio turnover is effectively zero, eliminating the invisible trading friction and contango risk found in futures-based alternatives. As a physical commodity wrapper, the fund mechanically generates a `0.00%` SEC yield, meaning the management fee acts as a pure structural drag on spot performance. From a tax perspective, the grantor trust structure avoids complex partnership paperwork, but capital gains are subject to the IRS collectibles tax rate mentioned above—which is notably higher than the standard long-term equity capital gains rate of `15–20%`.

State Street operates as the issuer, bringing tier-one institutional custody scale that is critical for auditing physical vaults. Launched in mid-2018, the trust has built a `7.9-year` operational track record with a perfectly stable mandate. The listed manager tenure mirrors the fund's exact age; however, because the trust is a static physical vault, active management continuity is irrelevant. The massive asset trajectory entirely neutralizes any closure risk.

The product's main strengths are its rock-bottom holding cost and vast execution liquidity, making it incredibly cheap to hold long-term. The primary drawback is the structural lack of yield combined with the elevated tax drag in non-sheltered accounts. For retail investors, this ETF is a direct replacement for GLD (`0.40%`); opting for this cheaper sibling sacrifices only the extreme options-chain depth utilized by institutional day-traders. Overall, this ETF's cost profile looks strong because it delivers the exact same physical spot exposure as legacy giants but at a permanently lowered price point.
