## Key Risk Factors Driving Further Downside

1. **Memory cost inflation** — ~2pp gross margin headwind starting Q2 2026. Management says fully mitigated within the year, but timing gap creates earnings risk.
2. **Tariff exposure** — ZBRA manufactures in China/Asia. Current tariffs "fully mitigated," but escalation risk remains.
3. **Leverage above comfort zone** — Debt/EBITDA at 3.01x, just above the 3.0x threshold. Any EBITDA miss pushes this higher and raises credit downgrade risk.
4. **Weak liquidity** — Quick ratio 0.52, current ratio 0.97. Below standard thresholds.
5. **ROIC barely above cost of capital** — At 8.02%, any earnings deterioration pushes ZBRA into value-destruction territory.
6. **Analyst downgrades accelerating** — Baird cut target from $350 to $300 (stock already at $202, so more cuts likely coming).
