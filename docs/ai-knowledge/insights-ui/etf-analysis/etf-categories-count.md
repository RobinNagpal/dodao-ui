# ETF Categories — Count & Summary

A quick-reference list of the top 15 ETF categories with the approximate number of US-listed ETFs in each, sourced from ETF Database (etfdb.com) as of early 2026. Counts reflect ETFdb's "best fit" 1:1 categorization where each ETF belongs to exactly one category.

## Notes

- Counts are approximate and sourced from ETF Database (etfdb.com) category pages as of early 2026.
- ETFdb uses a "1:1 best fit" methodology — each ETF is assigned to exactly one category.
- The Dividend/Equity Income count (~130) is estimated since ETFdb splits dividend strategies across multiple sub-categories.
- The Thematic/Megatrend count (~250) aggregates multiple ETFdb sub-categories (AI, clean energy, cannabis, cybersecurity, robotics, etc.) since no single screener category captures all thematic funds.
- Categories with the fewest ETFs (Mid Cap Blend at ~21, Commodities at ~34) tend to be dominated by a handful of large funds with the rest being niche or low-AUM alternatives.
