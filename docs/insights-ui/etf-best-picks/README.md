# Best ETF Picks — by Group

Curated shortlist of the 2 best ETFs under each group shown on
[koalagains.com/etfs/groups](https://koalagains.com/etfs/groups).

Picks are **not** derived from KoalaGains' internal scoring — they are independent
opinions based on the underlying benchmark index, the forward demand for the stocks in
that index, and how the specific ETF wrapper compares to its competitors tracking the
same (or a very similar) index.

## Selection framework

For each group we ask:

1. **Which benchmark index is the right one to own?** Indexes differ in coverage,
   methodology, and quality. We pick the index with the strongest long-term setup
   before picking the ETF wrapper.
2. **What's the future demand for the stocks inside that index?** A great wrapper
   over a fading benchmark is still a fading bet.
3. **How does the ETF "beat" its index?** Price indexes don't include dividends; the
   ETF holds the real stocks and passes dividends through, so the ETF's total return
   exceeds the headline price-index return by the dividend yield minus fees.
4. **Why this ETF over competitors that own the same things?** Fees, tracking quality,
   liquidity, tax structure, and AUM all matter when two ETFs hold near-identical
   baskets.
